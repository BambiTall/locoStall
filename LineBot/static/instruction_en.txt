Locostall Menu Button & Service Instructions:

• Language Setting (Bottom Left)🌐
Choose Chinese, Japanese or English,
Easily switch the screen language!

• Explore (Middle)🔍
Wondering what night market food to eat?
Click "Explore" to see what popular foods are there!

• Photo Ordering (Bottom Right)📸
Click the button and open the camera, aim at the merchant's sign,
And get the online ordering link!

• AI Robot🤖
Enter "@bot+question"
And the AI robot will answer any travel questions.
First time using? Try the reference questions below:
@bot What are some popular night markets in Taipei?
@bot What are the operating hours for Taipei night markets?
